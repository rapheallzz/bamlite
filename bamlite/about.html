Building the Future of Black Audience Advertising

BAM was created to address a fundamental gap in media: Black audiences lead in streaming behavior, yet remain underrepresented in how media investment is allocated.

We provide structured, programmatic access to verified Black audiences—enabling advertisers to transact with greater precision while ensuring publishers are properly represented within the media ecosystem.

Barbara Shaibu

Founder, Black Audience Marketplace (BAM) | Principal, Békwu Consults

Barbara Shaibu is a strategic media executive and sales innovator who has spent more than a decade bridging audience insights, cultural intelligence, and ad-tech performance to drive measurable growth.

As the Founder of Black Audience Marketplace (BAM) and Principal at Békwu Consults, Barbara leads a new movement in programmatic equity—connecting advertisers to verified Black audiences through data-driven, transparent, and scalable media execution.

Before founding Békwu, Barbara spent over a decade at BET Networks and Nielsen, where she advised on $100MM+ portfolios, developed strategic research frameworks, and helped major networks and advertisers translate data into market growth.

Known for her ability to turn complex analytics into compelling sales narratives, she has built a reputation for aligning culture and commerce—helping media brands and marketers better reach, value, and invest in the audiences that shape global influence.

Barbara is driven by a simple belief: when cultural data meets commercial accountability, representation becomes measurable—and monetizable.

Geoff Kowalski

CTV Monetization & Programmatic Infrastructure Partner

Geoff Kowalski is the Founder of Kodiak Media Group and a cross-platform media operator with over a decade of experience building programmatic infrastructure and driving revenue across broadcast, syndication, OTT, and digital video.

He also serves as Chief Revenue Officer at On The Spot Media (OTSM), where he leads revenue strategy, ad delivery systems, and cross-platform operations supporting large-scale media distribution. His work spans FAST channel monetization, supply-side infrastructure, and end-to-end CTV execution.

Through Kodiak Media Group, Geoff supports BAM’s marketplace infrastructure—enabling scalable access, efficient supply paths, and programmatic execution across streaming environments.

Geoff is a trusted partner to networks, studios, agencies, and publishers navigating the evolving CTV and programmatic landscape.
