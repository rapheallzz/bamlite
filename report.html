Why This Research Matters

Across music, streaming, film, and digital media, the global Black diaspora sits at the center of many of these cultural movements. Yet industry frameworks frequently treat Black audiences as a single demographic category rather than a global network shaped by migration, identity, and cross-border cultural exchange.
