      About BAM

      Our Vision

      Building the Future of Black Audience Advertising

      BAM was created to address a fundamental gap in media: Black audiences lead in streaming behavior, yet remain underrepresented in how media investment is allocated. We provide structured, programmatic access to verified Black audiences—enabling advertisers to transact with greater precision while ensuring publishers are properly represented within the media ecosystem.

      Leadership & Execution

      Founder-led strategy, powered by enterprise infrastructure

      Barbara Shaibu

      Founder, BAM | Principal, Békwu Consults

      Barbara Shaibu is a strategic media executive and sales innovator who has spent more than a decade bridging audience insights, cultural intelligence, and ad-tech performance to drive measurable growth. As the Founder of Black Audience Marketplace (BAM) and Principal at Békwu Consults, Barbara leads a new movement in programmatic equity—connecting advertisers to verified Black audiences through data-driven, transparent, and scalable media execution.

      Before founding Békwu, Barbara spent over a decade at BET Networks and Nielsen, where she advised on $100MM+ portfolios, developed strategic research frameworks, and helped major networks and advertisers translate data into market growth. Known for her ability to turn complex analytics into compelling sales narratives, she has built a reputation for aligning culture and commerce—helping media brands and marketers better reach, value, and invest in the audiences that shape global influence.
